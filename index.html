ABOUT IEC FABCHEM

IEC undertakes industrial process systems on turnkey basis in Power, Water and Chemical sectors. Our scope includes Design, Engineering, Manufacture, Supply, Erection, Testing and Commissioning. We have a successful track record of sixty years. Our technologies & systems are capable of fulfilling the industrial requirements and are backed by unrivalled application expertise and technical support. We strive to meet & exceed customer expectations consistently through our Quality Management System as per ISO 9001 : 2015, ISO 14001 : 2015 and OHSAS 18001 : 2007
